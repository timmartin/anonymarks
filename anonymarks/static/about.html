How it works

No account needed

You don't need to sign up for an account to store bookmarks here, or give us any information about yourself. You just need a password or phrase to identify yourself. This can be anything you like, as long as other people can't guess it.

You may find it's easier to use a short phrase rather than a single word. Perhaps pick a sentence at random from a book. But don't use anything predictable like a well-known saying.

Enter your password for access

Any computer or smartphone. Anywhere you have internet access. No software needed. Just enter your password and you're ready to go.

Bookmark what you like

You can bookmark anything that has a URL. Nobody else can see your bookmarks, and even if they did there would be no way to know you posted them.

We don't try to track you

We have no interest in tracking what you store, and no interest in making it easy for anyone else to track you. Find more about privacy and security in our FAQ.
